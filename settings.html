Lab Delivery Settings
How requisitions are sent to labs — no lab onboarding required

Requify delivers to labs automatically using their existing contact methods. Labs don't need to be on Requify to receive requisitions.

eFax (Recommended)
Delivered via Documo API to the lab's fax number. Works with all labs, no setup required. Average delivery: under 30 seconds.
Email
PDF attached and sent to the lab's intake email. Best for digital-forward labs.
Both (eFax + Email)
Maximum redundancy. Recommended for STAT requisitions.
